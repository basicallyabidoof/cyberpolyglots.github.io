About the Village

Welcome to the Cyberpolyglots Village! We are a community dedicated to bridging the gap between spoken languages and cybersecurity. Our mission is to unify multilingual professionals and enthusiasts, fostering global collaboration and knowledge sharing in the infosec space.

Activities

Hacker Peligro

A Hacker Jeopardy-style competition where Lintile will challenge you to translate key technical words within your answers.

Networking Sessions

Connect and share experiences with other polyglots in the cybersecurity community.

Threat Actor Research

Collaborative multi-lingual research sessions focusing on threat actors, analyzing primary sources in their native tongues.
